Graduation Day 2025

SMVITM celebrated its 12th Graduation Day on October 11, 2025, honoring top-performing students and VTU rank holders. The event featured distinguished guests including Dr. Vidyashankar S, Vice Chancellor of VTU, and Sri Suyog Shetty, CEO of Niveus Solutions.

Campus Achievements

SMVITM has been granted UGC Autonomous status for 10 years starting AY 2025–26. The institute also boasts NBA accreditation for CSE, ECE, and Mechanical Engineering, and continues to rank in the NIRF Innovation band 151–300.
